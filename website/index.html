The Problem

The prompt given to us by our client, Teletracking, emphasized the role of hospitals in disaster situations. Specifically the fact that thereâ€™s a large surge of non-patients seeking shelter as hospitals are viewed as safe places and generally have a backup source of power. They wanted us to create a solution that would help citizens, including those with medical needs and their caretakers, better handle disasters as well as help alleviate the inflow situation in hospitals during disasters. This should, ideally, take the form of an app for citizens as well as data solutions for hospital employees in disasters.

Our Solution

The App

The core of our solution is indeed a mobile application. This app helps people prepare for when a diaster strike and deal with it while it's happening. It includes preparation tools, shelter registration, and evacuation routes.
